What is Style-IN?

Style-IN is an intelligent and interactive fashion recommendation platform tailored to real-time weather conditions. It combines the beauty of fashion with the power of machine learning.

Just input live weather values—temperature, humidity, and wind speed—and let Style-IN guide you with the perfect outfit based on whether it’s sunny, rainy, winter, autumn, spring, or monsoon. A vibrant visual page will show clothing suggestions suited to the environment, ensuring you feel comfortable, confident, and fashionable all day.
